Our Story

Platform No.5 is a charming café nestled in the heart of Glenfield, directly opposite the train station. Our name pays homage to our railway-side location, creating a perfect spot for commuters and locals alike to enjoy quality food and beverages.

Since opening our doors, we've been dedicated to creating a warm, welcoming atmosphere where the community can gather, connect, and enjoy delicious food made with love and care.

What started as a small café has grown into a beloved local institution, known for our friendly service, quality ingredients, and commitment to the Glenfield community.

Our Philosophy

At Platform No.5, we believe that good food brings people together. Our philosophy is simple:

  • Quality Ingredients: We source the freshest, highest-quality ingredients to create our delicious menu items.
  • Community Focus: We're proud to be a gathering place for the Glenfield community, supporting local events and initiatives.
  • Exceptional Service: Our friendly staff aims to make every visit memorable with attentive, personalized service.
  • Sustainability: We strive to minimize our environmental footprint through responsible practices.
